# School Module

## Objective

Manage all school information and configuration.

---

## Users

* Super Admin
* School Admin

---

## Features

### School Registration

* Create School
* Edit School
* Suspend School
* Activate School

---

### Branding

* School Logo
* School Name
* Theme Colors
* Report Templates
* Certificate Templates

---

### Configuration

* Academic Years
* ID Generator
* Language
* Time Zone
* Date Format
* Currency

---

### Feature Management

Enable or Disable:

* Parent Portal
* Teacher Portal
* Student Portal
* Attendance
* Examination
* AI
* Notifications

---

## Success Criteria

A newly registered school should be fully operational in less than 30 minutes.
