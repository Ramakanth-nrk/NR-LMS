# Roles & Permissions

## Super Admin

Can manage:

* Schools
* School Admins
* System Settings
* Subscription Plans
* Branding Defaults

---

## School Admin

Can manage:

* Teachers
* Students
* Parents
* Academic Years
* Classes
* Subjects
* School Branding
* School Settings

---

## Teacher

Can:

* Create Exams
* Evaluate Answers
* View Students
* Upload Study Material
* Manage Attendance

---

## Student

Can:

* Take Exams
* View Results
* Practice Questions
* Earn Badges
* View Reports

---

## Parent

Can:

* View Child Progress
* Download Reports
* Receive Notifications
* Communicate with Teachers
