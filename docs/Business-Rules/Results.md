# Business Rules: Grading & Results

1. **Auto-Grading Rules**: Objective question types (MCQs, True/False, Fill-in-the-Blank, Match) must be graded by the backend immediately on submission.
2. **Subjective Grading Queue**: Short and long answer questions are marked as pending and queued for manual teacher evaluation.
3. **Class Rankings**: Rank must be calculated dynamically based on total score obtained, with tie-breakers using submission timestamps (earlier submission ranks higher).
4. **Publish Schedule**: Detailed results, correct answers, and rankings are only revealed to students after the teacher publishes the graded results.
